## Publication Format

The publication format is plaintext-first.

Each post is stored as a `post.txt` file and rendered into a shared HTML layout by the post page.

Posts may also be distributed in additional formats, including:

- Markdown
- PDF
- EPUB
- JSON
- Source archives
- Checksums
- OpenPGP signatures
- Bitcoin timestamp records

Those formats should only be advertised when the corresponding files actually exist.

## Purpose

The purpose of this archive is to preserve more than final products.

It records the reasoning, failures, revisions, experiments, architecture decisions, constraints, and discoveries behind the work.

A finished system may show what was built.

A development record shows why it was built, how it changed, what failed, and what remains unresolved.
