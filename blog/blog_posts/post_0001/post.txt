## Publication Philosophy

This archive follows a plaintext-first publishing model.

Every article is authored as a simple UTF-8 plaintext document stored as:

```text
post.txt
```

The surrounding HTML page simply loads the metadata and renders the plaintext into the common website layout.

Keeping the canonical publication in plaintext provides several advantages:

- Long-term preservation
- Human readability
- Version-control friendliness
- Minimal dependencies
- Easy cryptographic verification
- Portability across operating systems
- Simple archival
- Future-proof storage

When additional publication formats exist they may include:

- Markdown
- HTML
- PDF
- EPUB
- DOCX
- JSON metadata
- Source archives
- SHA-256
- SHA3-256
- OpenPGP signatures
- Bitcoin timestamp records

These artifacts should only be linked when they actually exist alongside the post.

## Why Keep a Development Journal?

Completed software rarely explains itself.

Repositories show commits.

Releases show features.

Neither adequately documents the reasoning behind architectural decisions, discarded approaches, experimental branches, failed ideas, implementation constraints, or changing objectives.

This archive exists to preserve that context.

The intention is to leave behind a complete engineering record rather than only finished software.

Over time these entries should demonstrate not only what was built, but how ideas evolved from initial concepts through research, implementation, testing, revision, and eventual deployment.

Some projects will succeed.

Others will fail.

Both outcomes have educational value.

## Looking Forward

The work documented here is expected to span many years.

As additional projects mature, this archive will expand alongside them, providing technical notes, engineering reports, research journals, implementation walkthroughs, release announcements, architectural discussions, and occasional observations from work conducted in the field.

The goal is not merely to publish software, but to preserve the complete history surrounding its creation.
