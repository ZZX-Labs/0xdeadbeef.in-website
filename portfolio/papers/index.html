Research, Design Documents, and Technical Whitepapers

This section archives deep technical writing authored by [0xdeadbeef], ranging from peer-reviewed research and internal whitepapers to speculative design notes on AI, Bitcoin systems, cyberwarfare, security frameworks, and socio-technical structures.



  • Cryptainer White Paper
    A layered encryption and access control methodology using animated sentinels (GKS), polyhedral entropy logic, and ECDSA-based key control architecture.
  • ZIRA White Paper
    Architectural proposal and design model for an adaptive AI assistant trained on localized, sovereign datasets. Describes GPT customization, mode switching, and domain-specific intelligence.
  • BitTick White Paper
    Technical blueprint for a globally weighted Bitcoin price ticker system sourcing real-time data from the top 100 exchanges with volume normalization and no third-party aggregation dependencies.
  • Nandi OS White Paper
    A cyberwarfare-hardened operating system modeled after Solaris, built atop Kali Linux, focused on field-deployable tech suites for researchers, operators, and defense analysts.
  • SpecieDex White Paper
    Conceptual and structural notes on creating a decentralized species database, combining digital field notes, AI classification, local-first data logging, and offline synchronization.
