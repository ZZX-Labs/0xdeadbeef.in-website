Music

Sound Design, EDM Production, & Audio Tooling

This section catalogs [0xdeadbeef]'s original music projects, modular audio systems, DAW environments, mastering workflows, and synthesis experimentation. From high-resolution sample libraries to real-time performance systems, the work blends hardware with code, signal with structure.



  • DELTREE Studios
    The private audio engineering and mastering studio for EDM, ambient, industrial, and experimental genres. Handles all sound design, production, and mastering internally.
  • beefTTS / oxear.py / audiosieve.py
    Original audio tools for TTS voice manipulation, entropy sampling from audio waveforms, and library tagging + playlist automation using FFmpeg, PyQt5, and python-vlc.
  • Modular Hardware Integration
    Configurations for integrating Moog, Korg, OP-1, Nord, Access Virus Ti2, and eurorack devices into digital workflows. Real-time MIDI + CV routing + sampling pipelines.
  • Ableton / Push3 Setup
    Custom RACKS, devices, and routing presets for a full Ableton suite configuration with Push3. Integrated with VLC_BOX project for decentralized DJ/VJ interfaces.
  • Sample Library Engineering
    Custom sound packs, presets, and curated loop packs designed from analog synth captures, field recordings, and downsampled lo-fi sequences.
