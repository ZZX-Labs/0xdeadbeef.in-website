Art

Visual Works, Studio Studies, Printworks & Digital Canvas

This section showcases an evolving archive of visual artwork created by [0xdeadbeef] across disciplines: drawing, painting, digital illustration, printmaking, and conceptual graphic design. Much of the early work began during time at Temple University’s Tyler School of Art and evolved into private studies, commissions, and experimental work in color theory, geometry, and semiotics.



  • Mr. Balaclava Studios
    The internal codename for [0xdeadbeef]’s personal visual studio — focusing on print, sketch, poster art, mythological symbolism, cryptographic iconography, and visual logic systems.
  • Glyph Key Sprite Iconography
    Series of pixel, vector, and animated sprite designs used in Cryptainer and other security visualization tools. Each GKS is a symbolic visual container for entropy and encrypted identity.
  • Poster & Propaganda Series
    Experimental art posters reflecting resistance aesthetics, post-collapse cyberpunk styles, Buddhist philosophical principles, and anti-totalitarian design language.
  • Ink & Paper Series
    Original pen and ink works from travel periods through San Francisco, Himachal Pradesh, NYC, and Scranton. Works explore geometry, form, chaotic energy, and sacred patterning.
  • Symbol Font Projects
    Original TrueType/OpenType font development projects including ideographic systems, cryptographic symbols, and ancient script reinterpretations for modern use.
