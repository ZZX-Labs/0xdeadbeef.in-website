Artificial Intelligence

Models, Agents, Assistants, and Tooling

The AI portfolio highlights projects involving GPT fine-tuning, transformer-based assistants, multi-modal AI tooling, automation agents, and deep learning research designed for practical deployment and sovereign infrastructure.



  • ZIRA (Zero-trust Interface Recon Assistant)
    An adaptive GPT-style AI assistant built for R&D and national defense analytics. Fine-tuned on private ZZX-Labs datasets with toggleable domain-specific expertise.
  • Hana
    AI voice assistant with multilingual capabilities, anime-inspired character design, and real-time voice synthesis and control via PyQt5, Mozilla TTS, and TensorFlow.
  • beefTTS
    A flexible text-to-speech system that allows custom voice modeling, pitch/formant control, and batch export of documents and books into MP3/WAV using AI voice profiles.
  • Cryptainer Glyph Engine
    An AI-backed generation system for creating one-of-a-kind animated sentinels (GKS) that guard Bitcoin containers. Traits are assigned via dice entropy and AI templates.
