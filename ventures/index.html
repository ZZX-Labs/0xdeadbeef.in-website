An evolving network of technical, commercial, research, and creative organizations.

Operating Principles

Principles guiding venture development.

Build real capability

Ventures should create durable technical, scientific, operational, educational, or institutional capacity.

Prefer sovereignty

Systems should reduce unnecessary dependence on centralized platforms, opaque infrastructure, custodians, and extractive intermediaries.

Use Bitcoin as infrastructure

Where appropriate, ventures should support Bitcoin-native payments, treasury, funding, settlement, accounting, and long-term reserves.

Document the work

Research, architecture, decisions, testing, provenance, limitations, ownership, licensing, and revision history should be preserved.

Separate public and private systems

Open-source work, commercial work, internal systems, restricted programs, client work, and sensitive research require distinct controls.

Develop for the long term

Ventures should prioritize maintainability, resilience, interoperability, security, institutional memory, and generational continuity.
