Profile

A multidisciplinary independent practice.

0xdeadbeef is an independent technologist, artist, engineer, researcher, writer, and founder whose work crosses technical, creative, scientific, cultural, and strategic disciplines.

The practice began in visual art and expanded through music, computing, software, electronics, cryptography, Bitcoin, artificial intelligence, machine learning, cybersecurity, OSINT, investigations, botany, photography, writing, and long-term organizational development.

The central method is consistent across every field: observe carefully, investigate the real system, separate evidence from assumption, design deliberately, build working tools, test failure conditions, document the result, and preserve the knowledge.

Art remains the conceptual foundation. Code, interfaces, circuit boards, data systems, writing, sound, mechanical form, and organizational structures are treated as variations of the same creative and analytical practice.

Origins

Fine art as the first systems language.

The earliest sustained work began in drawing, painting, composition, color, form, narrative, critique, photography, and visual communication. Modernist, contemporary, figurative, conceptual, and design traditions provided the first framework for understanding structure and meaning.

Visual practice trained attention toward proportion, rhythm, hierarchy, negative space, material, repetition, contrast, and perception. Those same concerns later became central to interface design, software architecture, PCB layout, information visualization, product design, photography, and technical writing.

Creative work was never treated as decoration applied after engineering. It became the architecture through which engineering problems were interpreted.

Every application has composition. Every circuit has rhythm. Every dataset has form. Every protocol imposes a visual and conceptual model on the person who must understand and operate it.

Music

Sound as engineering, composition, and research.

Music became a major creative and technical discipline through digital audio workstations, sampling, synthesis, composition, sequencing, recording, sound design, mixing, performance, DJ systems, and audio electronics.

Analog recordings, digital samples, MIDI orchestration, software instruments, field audio, and visualized waveforms created a bridge between traditional art practice and computational systems.

Music production reinforced an understanding of timing, modular structure, signal flow, layered systems, dynamic range, automation, latency, and human-machine interaction.

These lessons later influenced software interfaces, visualizers, hardware controls, embedded devices, event-driven architecture, and experimental generative systems.

India

Travel extended across India through Maharashtra, Gujarat, Rajasthan, Punjab, Himachal Pradesh, Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, West Bengal, Sikkim, Karnataka, Goa, Tamil Nadu, Kerala, and additional regions.

The experience included major cities, pilgrimage centers, rural communities, mountain regions, forests, deserts, coastlines, industrial corridors, educational institutions, and sacred sites.

This period established a lifelong interest in India, South Asia, the Himalayan region, Buddhism, regional security, political economy, infrastructure, energy, industrial capacity, technology sovereignty, trade, language, and cultural continuity.

The experience created a lasting belief that technological independence requires control over energy, manufacturing, communications, computation, capital, software, hardware, semiconductors, education, and institutional knowledge.

Spiritual and Philosophical Life

Buddhist practice, systems thinking, and ethical restraint.

A practicing Buddhist since youth, 0xdeadbeef has studied Buddhist philosophy and visited major sacred sites including Sarnath and Bodh Gaya.

The spiritual framework emphasizes attention, interdependence, impermanence, compassion, disciplined observation, nonviolence, responsibility, and the consequences of action.

These principles influence the use of technical power. Capability does not automatically justify deployment. Knowledge should be governed by context, proportionality, authorization, and the duty to avoid unnecessary harm.

The preferred role of technology is to reduce coercion, preserve agency, improve understanding, strengthen resilience, and expand the ability of individuals and communities to control their own systems.
